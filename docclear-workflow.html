Cross-Module Automation & Impact
⚙️
Service Completed → Invoice Auto-Created
When a service order is marked Completed, a Draft invoice is instantly generated with the service's sell price pre-filled as a line item.
Service Order → Invoice (Draft)
🧾
Invoice Paid → Wallet Income Transaction
Marking an invoice as Paid creates a Wallet Income transaction for the selected account (Cash or Bank), updating the running balance automatically.
Invoice Paid → Wallet +Income
💳
Expense Paid → Wallet Deduction
When an expense is marked Paid, the amount is automatically deducted from the chosen account, maintaining a real-time accurate balance.
Expense Paid → Wallet −Deducted
💬
Customer Phone → All WhatsApp Actions
The WhatsApp number stored on the customer profile powers every communication touchpoint: document reminders, invoice shares, and direct messages — zero re-entry.
Customer Phone → wa.me links everywhere
📊
Documents → Dashboard Expiry Counters
Dashboard "Expiring Soon" card reads live from the documents table. Clicking it navigates directly to the Documents page filtered by critical/warning status.
Documents → Dashboard counters
📈
All Modules → Reports Aggregation
Reports read across Invoices (revenue), Expenses (costs), Service types (breakdown by category), and Customers (top spenders) to compute profit and trends.
All modules → Reports dashboard
